Case Study

Leafio — Medical Cannabis Brand & Campaign

Creative Strategy Branding Photography Creative Direction

Context

Leafio is a national medical cannabis platform operating across a network of 4,000+ pharmacies in Australia. The brand required a full creative transformation — building trust and authority in a highly regulated pharmaceutical category where conventional advertising is strictly restricted, and patient education is the primary lever for growth.

Approach

I led the creative strategy and direction across the full brand ecosystem — refining the identity, developing campaign creative, producing clinical and lifestyle photography, and designing publications for both patient and prescriber audiences. A targeted B2B lead generation campaign was built to engage healthcare professionals, using long-form editorial content and educational assets designed to build credibility and drive meaningful engagement within pharmaceutical advertising constraints.

Impact

The B2B lead gen campaign delivered an average session duration of 5 minutes 9 seconds — far exceeding industry benchmarks — demonstrating that the content strategy successfully engaged healthcare professionals at depth. The refreshed brand identity and publication system positioned Leafio as a credible, trusted authority within the medical cannabis space.
