Case Study

Natural Beauty — Brand, Packaging & Ecommerce

Brand Development Packaging Ecommerce Photography

Context

Natural Beauty is a marine collagen startup requiring a complete brand built from the ground up — identity, premium packaging, a Shopify ecommerce store with membership and rewards functionality, and a photography strategy to position the brand in the premium health and wellness space.

Approach

I developed the brand identity from scratch, designed premium packaging systems from concept through to press-ready artwork, and built the Shopify ecommerce experience with integrated membership and loyalty rewards. Product and lifestyle photography was art directed to balance clinical credibility with aspirational wellness positioning.

Impact

The cohesive brand system established Natural Beauty as a premium player in the marine collagen category. The Shopify store launched with strong conversion metrics and the membership programme drove repeat purchases from launch.
