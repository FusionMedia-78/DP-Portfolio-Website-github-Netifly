Capabilities

What I do.

Creative Direction

End-to-end creative leadership from concept through production. I build and direct teams, define the creative vision, and ensure every deliverable meets the strategic brief — whether that's a national TV campaign, a product launch, or a complete brand overhaul.

Brand & Campaign

Brand strategy, identity systems, and campaign development that connect with audiences and drive results. From complete rebrands generating 400% sales lifts to building brand guidelines from the logo up, I make brands that work harder.

Photography

Two decades behind the lens — from studio product shoots to large-scale commercial productions with crews of up to one hundred. I specialise in food and beverage, lifestyle, product, and campaign photography that serves a clear creative and commercial purpose.

Digital & Ecommerce

Figma-designed, Shopify-built ecommerce experiences that convert. I handle the full creative stack — design systems, UI/UX prototyping, custom front-end components, and performance-ready creative optimised for paid social channels.

Film & Content

Documentary, campaign videos, explainer content, and animated storyboards. From investor-facing documentaries to 30-second product spots featuring celebrity talent, I direct video content that educates, persuades, and engages.

Packaging & Print

Packaging design from concept sketches through to structural dielines and press-ready artwork. I've created packaging systems for DTC health and beauty brands, point-of-sale suites for theme parks, and a pharmacy industry publication from scratch.
