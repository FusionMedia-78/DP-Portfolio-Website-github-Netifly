Case Study

Village Roadshow — F&B Creative

F&B Branding Creative Direction Photography

Context

Village Roadshow Theme Parks operates six major entertainment venues on the Gold Coast — Sea World, Movie World, Wet'n'Wild, Paradise Country, Outback Spectacular, and TopGolf. Over fifteen years as Senior Creative and Lead Photographer, I held full creative ownership across 30+ food and beverage outlets.

Approach

I helped scale the in-house creative studio from a 4-person team to an 18-person internal agency, replacing all external agencies. My work covered end-to-end F&B creative: stakeholder consultation, venue theming, logo design, menu board systems, food photography, and architectural illustration. I also directed creative for a Lionsgate theme park food and beverage rollout in China — 4 outlets designed within Lionsgate brand guidelines.

Impact

Delivered 200+ campaigns across TV, cinema, social, print, and OOH over 15+ years. Directed shoot teams of up to 100 crew and talent. The in-house model replaced all external agencies, saving significant costs while improving creative consistency and turnaround across six parks. Campaigns ran nationally and internationally across Australia, the USA, and China.
