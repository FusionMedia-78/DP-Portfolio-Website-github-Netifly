Case Study

Riper.io — Blockchain Food Supply Chain

Creative Strategy Branding UI/UX Illustration

Context

Riper.io is a blockchain-based agriculture platform focused on food supply chain traceability. The startup needed a complete brand identity, UI/UX design, and go-to-market creative that could communicate a complex technology proposition to both investors and end users.

Approach

I branded the platform from scratch — developing the complete identity system, designing the UI/UX in Figma, creating animated isometric illustrations to visualise the supply chain, and producing all go-to-market creative. The visual language needed to bridge the gap between agricultural simplicity and blockchain technology.

Impact

The brand system gave Riper.io a distinctive position in the agri-tech space, helping the team communicate its value proposition clearly to investors and potential partners. The isometric illustration style became the signature visual element of the brand across all touchpoints.
