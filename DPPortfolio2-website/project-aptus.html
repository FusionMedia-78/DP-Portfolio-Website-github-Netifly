Case Study

Aptus Plant Tech — Global Rebrand & Ecommerce

Creative Strategy Branding Ecommerce Photography

Context

Aptus Plant Tech is a DTC plant nutrition brand operating across Australia, New Zealand, the US, and Canada. The brand needed a full rebrand and ecommerce relaunch to match the quality of its products and compete in a crowded market with strict advertising restrictions around cannabis-adjacent content.

Approach

I directed the complete rebrand — identity, packaging, product photography, and art direction. The Shopify ecommerce platform was relaunched across four international markets with Figma-designed UI components. Modular creative systems were built for Meta advertising under cannabis content restrictions, with testing-ready visual storytelling flows for performance creative.

Impact

The rebrand and relaunch delivered a 400% sales increase in the first month post-launch. The modular creative system enabled rapid iteration across paid social channels, and the new brand identity elevated Aptus from a niche player to a premium-positioned brand across four countries.
