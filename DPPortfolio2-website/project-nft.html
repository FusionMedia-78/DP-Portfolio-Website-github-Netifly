Case Study

NFT Visual Campaigns

NFT Design Creative Strategy Digital Art

Context

As part of the JYCO Startup Studio portfolio, I directed the visual campaign design and creative strategy for an NFT project launch. The challenge was creating a visual identity and campaign that could cut through a saturated market and drive rapid sell-through.

Approach

I developed the complete visual campaign — from character design and art direction through to community-facing creative and launch assets. The strategy combined digital art with a community-driven marketing approach, building anticipation through phased visual reveals and forum engagement.

Impact

The collection sold out in 30 minutes, reaching a $2M market cap and achieving a top 14 project ranking that held for 2+ weeks. The visual identity became a recognisable brand within the NFT community and drove sustained secondary market activity.
