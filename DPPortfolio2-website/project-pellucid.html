Case Study

Pellucid — Documentary, Brand & Product Launch

Documentary Video Production Branding Photography

Context

Pellucid, an Australian-Asian team developing plant-based THC-free cannabinoid products, needed creative assets that could educate high-value investors, build consumer confidence, and establish a premium brand presence. The pitch deck creative at JYCO Startup Studio contributed to securing $10M in investment capital.

Approach

I produced a documentary exploring the THC-free cannabis industry as a pre-meeting asset for investor engagement. A 30-second campaign video was directed featuring celebrity talent. The logo was refined to better reflect its core value of transparency. An explainer video made the science of the endocannabinoid system accessible to a general audience. All product photography balanced clinical credibility with lifestyle appeal.

Impact

The documentary and pitch materials played a direct role in securing $10M in capital. The explainer content simplified complex science for consumers, and the cohesive brand refresh positioned Pellucid as a credible, premium player in the emerging cannabinoid wellness category.
