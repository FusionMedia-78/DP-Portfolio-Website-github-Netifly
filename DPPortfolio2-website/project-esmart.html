Case Study

ESmart Farms — Sustainable Agriculture

Creative Direction Video Production Sustainability

Context

ESmart Farms is an off-grid sustainable farming concept with ambitions to present its model at UN-level forums. The project required creative direction that could communicate the vision of sustainable agriculture to high-level stakeholders and the broader public.

Approach

I directed the creative strategy for the project, including a video presentation targeted at the United Nations and educational content explaining the off-grid farming model. The creative needed to balance scientific credibility with accessible storytelling to reach diverse audiences.

Impact

The video and educational content provided ESmart Farms with presentation-ready materials suitable for international forums. The creative helped articulate a complex sustainability proposition in a way that resonated with both institutional stakeholders and general audiences.
