Case Study

Doomsday — Attraction Launch

Branding Photography Key Art

Context

Doomsday was a major new attraction launch for one of Village Roadshow's theme parks, requiring a complete brand identity, key art campaign photography, and visual development that could drive attendance across national media channels.

Approach

I directed the brand identity development, key art photography, and campaign visual development. The creative needed to capture the thrill and intensity of the ride experience while working across TV, cinema, print, OOH, social, and digital channels. Large-scale composite imagery was produced by blending on-ride photography with environmental elements.

Impact

The campaign launched nationally across multiple media channels, driving awareness and attendance for one of the park's most significant new attractions. The key art became an iconic piece in the park's marketing library and set the benchmark for future attraction launches.
